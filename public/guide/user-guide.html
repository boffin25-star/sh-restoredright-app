🌐App-Wide Features

🏠 Home

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Your landing screen — a grid of every tab you can access.

What it does

Home is the first screen you see after logging in. It shows every tab you have access to as a grid of tappable tiles — icon, label, and (when relevant) a red notification badge showing how many things need your attention.

Notification badges

The My Tasks tile shows a live count combining overdue follow-ups, new tasks assigned to you, change orders awaiting your approval, and purchase requests awaiting your approval. The Backup tile shows a reminder dot if you haven't backed up today.

Navigating from Home

1Tap any tile to open that tab.
2Once inside a tab, a scrollable icon bar appears at the top so you can jump to any other tab without returning Home first.
3Tap the 🏠 Home icon in that bar anytime to come back to the grid.
The Home grid and the top nav bar always show tabs in the same order, and both automatically hide tabs you don't have permission to see (like Company Info, which is admin-only).

🔑 Login & Splash Screen

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

How signing in works, and what happens the first time you open the app.

Splash screen

Opening the app shows the full S&H Services badge and a single Login button. Tapping Login takes you to the name-picker screen.

Picking your name

The login screen shows every team member as a tile in a grid — your initial in a navy circle, name underneath. Tap your name to sign in. There's no password — this is a trusted-device system.

Once you sign in on your phone, you stay signed in. You won't need to log in again unless you clear your browser data or switch devices.

✅ My Tasks

👤 Who can use this: Everyone (approvals visible to Admins only)

Every task assigned to you, plus anything waiting on your approval, all in one action queue.

Task subcategories

Tasks are grouped into collapsible sections by status:

  • 🆕 New — just assigned, not started
  • 🔧 In Progress — actively being worked
  • ⏸️ On-Hold — paused
  • ✅ Completed — done

Urgent tasks (overdue follow-up date, or marked urgent/high priority) float to the top of their group with a 🔥 URGENT ribbon.

Opening a task

Tap any task card to open the Task Detail view — a sheet that slides up from the bottom. From there you can see the full description, assignees, dates, and a comment thread. Status-change buttons let you move a task between New/In Progress/On-Hold/Completed, and every change is automatically logged as an activity entry so there's a history.

⏳ Pending Approvals — visible only to Brandon, Erik, and Matt. This subcategory sits at the top of My Tasks (auto-expanded when something's waiting) and shows two kinds of items needing sign-off:
  • 🔄 Change Orders — tap ✍️ Sign to Approve & Complete to open the signature pad, or Reject with a reason.
  • 💰 Large Purchase Requests (over $300) — tap ✓ Approve Purchase or Reject.
Approving from here updates the job automatically — no need to navigate into the job itself.

📋 Jobs

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Every job in the system, grouped by status, with a red flag on anything that needs attention.

How jobs are organized

Jobs are grouped into two top-level sections — 📋 Estimates and ✅ Contracted — each split further into status groups: New, Scheduled, In Progress, and Done. All groups start collapsed; tap a group header to expand it.

Search

Typing in the search box bypasses all the grouping and shows a flat list of matching jobs instantly.

The red "!" attention flag

A small red exclamation badge appears in the top-left corner of any job card where something needs action:

  • The job has an unpaid balance
  • A permit is required but hasn't been obtained yet
  • Any task on that job is still incomplete
Scan the Jobs list for red flags before you scan anything else — it tells you at a glance which jobs need something done, without opening each one.

Badges on each job card

Status badge (color-coded), 📋 Estimate badge if it's not yet a signed contract, 🏛️ Permit badge (red = needed, green = obtained), and payment badges (💰 Paid, ½ Deposit, or red Unpaid).

Opening a job

Tap any job card to open the full Job Detail view — this is the biggest screen in the app, covered in the next section.

📁 Job Detail — All Sections

👤 Who can use this: Everyone (dollar amounts hidden for some roles)

The complete record for one job: photos, contracts, permit, measurements, scheduling, tasks, change orders, receipts, hours, notes, links, and cost breakdown.

Editing basic job info

Tap ✏️ Edit in the header to make the customer name, address, job type, and other top-level fields editable. A 💾 Save button appears right next to Edit — tap it when you're done. Tap Cancel to discard changes.

The accordion sections

Below the header, every part of the job is organized into single-open accordion sections — each with its own circular navy icon matching the app's icon style. Tap a section header to expand it; opening one automatically closes whichever was open before, keeping the screen manageable.

📷Photos — camera-icon section for job photos, with notes per photo.
📝Contracts & Invoices — documents tied to this job.
🏛️Permit — permit documents, auto-saves on every add/remove.
📐Measurements — room/area measurements for the job.
📅Scheduling & Assignment — crew, dates, and assignment info.
✅Tasks — job-specific tasks with full edit/save/delete.
🔄Change Orders — see the dedicated section below.
🧾Receipts — see the dedicated section below.
⏱️Labor Hours — hours logged against the job.
🗒️Job Updates — a running note/activity log.
🔗Links & Resources — external links (Matterport, etc.) relevant to the job.
🧮Cost & Tax Breakdown — the built-in cost calculator, pre-filled from this job's data.

Change Orders — full approval workflow

Each change order has two independent approval tracks shown side-by-side: Client and 🏢 S&H Services. Each side has its own ✍️ Sign to Approve button (opens a signature pad) and a Reject option (captures a reason). Only Brandon, Erik, and Matt can approve the company side.

  • Change orders now include Estimated Hours and Estimated Completion Date fields, shown on the card and included in the signature summary.
  • A ⚠️ subject-to-change disclaimer appears on every card and on the signature pad itself when the client is signing — explaining that cost/hours/completion date are estimates, not guarantees.
  • The signature pad shows a legal disclaimer to the client before they sign, covering authorization to perform the work and payment terms.
  • Adding a change order automatically notifies Brandon, Erik, and Matt via My Tasks and the notification bell.
  • ✉️ Email and 💬 Text buttons send the client a summary including amount, hours, completion date, and the same disclaimer language.
  • The accordion badge shows amber "X pending", red "X rejected", or a green checkmark once fully approved.

Receipts — including large purchase approval

Add receipts with vendor, amount, category, photo, and travel time. Any receipt over $300 works differently:

1Fill in vendor and amount — once it crosses $300, the form switches modes automatically and a warning box appears.
2Tap Request Approval instead of Save. This creates a purchase request (not a receipt yet) and notifies Brandon, Erik, and Matt.
3A confirmation screen appears telling you not to make the purchase yet — wait for approval.
4Once an admin approves it (from My Tasks, the notification panel, or right here in Receipts), it moves to an "✅ Approved — Ready to Purchase" card.
5Make the purchase, then tap 📷 Add Receipt — Mark as Purchased to upload the actual receipt photo and complete the loop.
Purchases under $300 save immediately as a normal receipt — no approval needed.

➕ Add New Job

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Create a new job record from scratch, or fill it in instantly from an existing contact or a phone contact.

Filling in the client

Three ways to get client info into the form without typing it all manually:

  • 👥 From Contacts — search your saved Contacts list and tap one to auto-fill name, company, phone, email, and address.
  • 📱 From Phone — Android only. Opens your phone's native contact picker.
  • 📇 Upload vCard — works on any device, including iPhone. Share a contact from your Contacts app as a .vcf file and upload it here.
  • 📋 Paste Contact — paste any copied contact text and the app pulls out name, phone, and email automatically.

Job details

Job type, address, scope of work, estimated cost, priority, crew assignment, key dates, and — for insurance jobs — claim number and adjuster info. You can also mark whether a permit is needed and attach photos right from this form.

Submitting a new job takes you straight to the Jobs list afterward so you can see it appear in its status group.

📅 Schedule

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

A calendar view of every job's key dates.

What it shows

A calendar layout showing scheduled jobs by date — start dates, key milestones, and crew assignments. Use this to see what's happening on a given day at a glance, or to spot scheduling conflicts before they happen.

🧾 Receipts (Tab)

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Quick access point for receipt-related work — the same receipt logic used inside each Job Detail.

This tab surfaces receipt-related functionality at the top level. For full detail on how receipts and large-purchase approvals work, see the Job Detail → Receipts section above — the logic is identical whether you get there from a specific job or from this tab.

📝 Contracts

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Contract documents tied to jobs — creation, e-signature, and status tracking.

What it does

Manage contract documents for jobs — draft, send for signature, and track signed status. Contracts connect to the same client-facing signature flow used elsewhere in the app.

💰 Invoices

👤 Who can use this: Everyone (dollar amounts hidden for some roles)

Invoice creation and payment status tracking for completed work.

What it does

Create and track invoices tied to jobs, and monitor payment status (deposit received, final paid, etc.) — the same payment badges you see on the Jobs list are driven from here.

Clover payment-link integration is planned for a future update — customers will eventually be able to pay an invoice directly through a link generated from this tab.

📊 Going Rates

👤 Who can use this: Everyone (editing is Admin only)

A searchable, sortable reference list of current industry pricing by trade — including a direct Xactimate price list import.

Browsing rates

Rates are grouped by category — Roofing, Water Damage, Fire/Smoke Damage, Mold Remediation, General Labor, Renovation, Other. Use the search box to filter by work type, or tap a category pill to narrow the list. Each entry shows the price range, unit, source, and when it was last updated.

Refreshing

The 🔄 refresh button re-pulls the latest data from the database and updates the "Last updated" timestamp. It does not search the live web — a plain app can't safely do that. Rates are kept current by periodically asking Claude to research updated pricing, or by importing directly from Xactimate.

📥 Import Xactimate — admins can upload a Xactimate price list export (CSV or Excel). The app auto-detects Description, Unit, Price, Category, and Selector/Code columns from common export formats, shows a preview before saving anything, and lets you choose whether to replace existing rates in a category or add alongside them. Imported rates are tagged "Source: Xactimate price list" so you can always tell official pricing apart from general market research.

🧮 Cost Calculator

👤 Who can use this: Everyone (dollar amounts hidden for some roles)

A standalone profit/cost calculator supporting Fixed Contract and Time & Material billing modes.

How it works

Enter labor, materials, and extra costs manually, or — when accessed from inside a specific Job Detail — the calculator pre-fills automatically from that job's logged hours and receipts. Switch between Fixed Contract and Time & Material billing modes; the app remembers your choice per job.

What it calculates

  • Employee wages merged from logged labor hours (hours are locked, wages stay editable)
  • Materials cost breakdown, auto-populated from Materials-category receipts, with duplicate detection
  • Non-materials extra costs (permits, equipment rental, disposal, subcontractors)
  • Sales tax by ZIP code (Spokane-area rate table), WA B&O tax, L&I, use tax, overhead, and target margin — all adjustable
  • A pie chart visualizing the cost breakdown

Saving your work

When used inside a job, the calculator auto-saves 1 second after any edit, plus a manual Save Now button and a ↺ Reset All option. A Saved Snapshot panel lists every saved data point with individual clear buttons.

🎯 Sales Leads

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

An action-item queue for sales leads — sortable, filterable, with overdue follow-ups surfaced automatically.

The action queue

Leads default-sort by ⚡ Action Priority — overdue follow-ups rise to the top (most overdue first), then leads due today, then everything else, with closed leads (Won/Lost/Not Interested) sinking to the bottom. Open the tab and you immediately see what needs attention.

Other sort options

📅 Follow-Up Date (soonest first), 🆕 Newest First, 💰 Estimated Value (highest first), 🔤 Name (A–Z) — plus the existing search box, status filter, and salesperson filter, all of which combine with sorting.

Priority flags on cards

A red "!" badge appears on any lead with an overdue follow-up; an amber "•" badge marks follow-ups due today — so you can scan the list without reading every date.

Opening a lead

Tapping a lead goes straight into the edit view — no separate read-only screen first. A quick-glance strip at the top shows job type, estimated value, assigned salesperson, and follow-up date. Below that, the form is split into three collapsible sections (all collapsed by default): 👤 Contact Info, 🔧 Job Details, and 📈 Sales Tracking. Tap a section to expand just what you need to edit.

Importing a contact

Same three import options as Contacts and Add New Job: 📱 From Phone (Android), 📇 Upload vCard (any device), 📋 Paste Contact.

Map preview

Once an address is entered, a 📍 Open in Google Maps link and an embedded map preview appear automatically.

Import / Export via Excel

📥 Download exports all leads to an Excel file. 📤 Upload imports leads from a spreadsheet — a lead only needs a Contact Name or a Company name to import successfully, since business/referral leads (like property management companies) often don't have a specific person's name yet.

👥 Contacts

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Your master contact list — customers, vendors, adjusters, and anyone else you deal with regularly.

Adding a contact

Fill in the form manually, or use the same three import options: 📱 From Phone (Android), 📇 Upload vCard, or 📋 Paste Contact — all three funnel straight into the Name, Phone, Email, and Address fields with a confirmation toast showing what was imported.

Map preview

Once an address is entered, a 📍 Open in Google Maps link and embedded map preview appear in both the add/edit form and the contact's detail view.

Contact types

Each contact is tagged with a type (customer, vendor, adjuster, etc.) shown as a colored icon, so you can tell at a glance what kind of relationship it is.

⚙️ Admin

👤 Who can use this: Everyone can view; editing restricted

Team member management, password resets, and permission settings.

👥 Team Members

Add new employees (name + crew display name), edit existing ones, deactivate (soft-remove, preserves their history on old jobs), or fully remove someone. Inactive employees disappear from pickers everywhere else in the app but their historical data stays intact.

🔑 Password Reset

For each team member: generate a random password, or set a custom one (6+ characters). A 📋 Copy button puts it on the clipboard for easy sharing. The password is shown once in a monospace box.

💾 Backup

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Export a full backup of the app's data.

What it does

Creates a downloadable backup of the app's job and business data. A reminder badge appears on the Home tile and in the nav bar if you haven't backed up yet today — tapping into the Backup tab clears that reminder.

📁 Docs

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

General document storage for files that don't belong to a specific job.

What it does

A general-purpose file storage area for company documents that aren't tied to any one job — separate from the job-specific Contracts & Invoices and Permit sections inside individual jobs.

🏢 Company Info

👤 Who can use this: Admins Only (Brandon, Erik, Matt)

Official business identifiers (license, UBI, TIN/EIN) and a scannable QR business card.

This entire tab — and the sensitive data inside it — is only visible to Brandon, Erik, and Matt. Everyone else sees a simple "Admins Only" message if they somehow navigate here directly.

What's stored

  • Legal Business Name and DBA/Trade Name
  • Business License Number and expiration date
  • UBI Number and TIN/EIN — both masked by default (shown as dots) with a 👁️ Tap to reveal toggle for privacy
  • Company phone, email, address (with Google Maps link), and website
  • Free-text notes — good for insurance policy numbers, bonding info, or renewal reminders

Social Media links

An editable list — add, rename, or remove any platform link (LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and more) without needing a database change each time. Each link shows a small brand-colored icon.

📇 Scannable Business Card (QR Code)

Tap the QR code to enlarge it full-screen for someone else to scan with their phone camera. Scanning it opens a public web page — no login required — showing your logo, phone, email, address, website, and social media links, plus a 📇 Save Contact button that downloads a vCard so one tap adds S&H directly to their phone's contacts.

Because the public page pulls live from the same Company Info data, updating your phone number or adding a new social link here updates what everyone sees when they scan the code — no need to reprint anything.

🌐 App-Wide Features

👤 Who can use this: Everyone

Things that work the same way no matter which tab you're in.

🔔 Notifications

The bell icon shows a live count combining new jobs, new tasks, overdue follow-ups, @mentions, change orders awaiting approval, and purchase requests awaiting approval. Tapping it opens a panel where — for admins — change orders and purchase requests can be approved or rejected right there, without navigating anywhere else.

✍️ Signatures

Used for change order approval. The signature pad works with touch or mouse, and shows a legal disclaimer to the client before they sign, plus a summary of what's being approved (description, amount, estimated hours, completion date).

📱 Contact Import (everywhere)

Anywhere you can add a contact — Add New Job, Leads, Contacts — you get the same three import options: the native Android picker, vCard upload (works on iPhone too), and paste-to-parse. Phone and email fields are validated automatically: phone numbers auto-format as you type and letters get stripped out; emails are checked for a valid format on blur.

📍 Maps everywhere

Any address field — Contacts, Leads, Job Detail — shows a tap-to-open Google Maps link plus a small embedded map preview once you've typed an address.

🤖 Help Assistant

The floating button in the bottom corner (shown as the S&H badge) opens a help/chat assistant from anywhere in the app.

Desktop vs. Mobile view

A toggle in the header switches between a mobile-width layout and a wider desktop layout — your choice is remembered on that device.
